About Us

Based in Rajasthan, Arvind is a brand that grew out of a fascination for Indian textiles and an eagerness to convert them into tastefully designed, functional fashion accessories.
Meticulously handcrafted, our range of formal clutch purses, potlis, wallets, slings and tote bags are a testament to the virtuosity of our local artisans and their passion to bring into being our design concepts that are an amalgamation of traditional fabric and modern form.
